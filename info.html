Instructions:

1. This game makes you try to unscramble letters to form an acceptable english word according to the Collins scrabble dictionary

2. After clicking on one of the buttons below, you will be redirected to the 'play' page

3. You will see three words that are scrambled. You have to unscramble the words and enter them in the blank spaces next to their respetive labels

4. If you get all three words right, good job! You can replay the same game option or go back to the home page and click on a different option

5. If you get less than three words right, you will have to answer additional questions

8. You can play as many rounds as you want and have fun!
